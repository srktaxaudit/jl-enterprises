📦 Migrate from Vyapar

One-time opening-balance migration. Upload the vyapar-migration.json file, run a Dry-run first to reconcile the totals against your Vyapar reports, then Import. Brings in products & stock, party outstanding balances, and customer contacts (name / mobile / address). Transaction history stays in Vyapar. Re-running is safe (nothing is duplicated).

⚠️ Before you Import: take a Supabase backup of the database — that is your true undo. The Dry-run writes nothing.

Enter your actual current cash & bank balances (Vyapar doesn't store them). They flow into the opening trial balance; the rest lands in Capital.

Import ledgers (Chart of Accounts)

CSV columns: code, name, type, group, openingBalance, openingSide, gstRate, gstin, hsnCode. Type = ASSET / LIABILITY / EQUITY / INCOME / EXPENSE; side = DR / CR. A header row is optional. Existing codes are skipped.
