Warranty Policy

Manufacturer-backed, with our service support

1. Manufacturer warranty

Every product we sell carries the manufacturer's warranty as printed on the box/warranty card (typically 1 year on the product and longer on specific parts like a compressor, motor or panel). The exact period and terms are set by the brand.

2. Keep your invoice

Your JL Enterprises tax invoice is your proof of purchase and warranty start date. Please keep it safe — you can also download it any time from My Orders.

3. Raising a warranty claim

If a product develops a fault within warranty, contact us and we'll help you raise the request with the brand's authorised service centre and coordinate the visit. You can also book a visit directly on our Book Service page.

4. What's usually NOT covered

Physical damage, liquid damage, voltage-surge damage (use a stabilizer!), unauthorised repairs, and normal wear of consumables are generally outside warranty as per the brand's terms.

5. After warranty — we're still here

Once the warranty ends, our own service & AMC team can repair and maintain your appliance at fair rates. Ask about an Annual Maintenance Contract on the Service page.
